Goodrich, Michigan

Congradulations on reaching a population over 2,000 this year!

The village was incorporated in 1957, splitting off from Atlas Township. Located in Genesee County, you can leave this place by taking M-15 to I-69 and I-75. Over the years downtown had been described as “sleepy, but thriving” according to the official Village of Goodrich Website.
