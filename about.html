About Ashley

    I grew up in the small town of Goodrich, Michigan outside of Flint. My neighbor introduced me to the high school robotics team in the summer of 2010 and I loved that it was a mini business. The product was a robot that had to play a different game every year and the atmosphere at competitions was like a sporting event. I got to do a little of everything; work with power tools, work on the team's website, drive the robot at competitions, and helped the team win awards like Imagery, Automation in Control, & The Judge's award by talking to judges.

    I felt a lack of support as I explored these skills from the adults that were supposed to be my support network. After high school I went to Full Sail University and earned a Bachelor's Degree in Music Business. I continued to stay involved in FIRST Robotics as a business team mentor for Exploding Bacon #1902. More about that here. The experience working with FIRST Robotics teams led me to becoming the manager of The Robot Garage in Birmingham, MI. Then the pandemic hit and their physical location was closed for multiple years. In that time I started delivering groceries through Shipt and later Instacart to pay the bills. I also had a lot of free time to explore other skills including video editing and graphic design.

    The year 2023 I decided to start learning front end development with a free trial of LinkedIn Learning and came across Hackbright's Bright Path Full Stack Development Bootcamp sponsored by Perficient. I created my first full stack application - Whimify. I am currently finishing the Bright Path program with a specialization in Java.
